Loop of Life

// ACRYLIC LUMINESCENT · 40" × 60" · UV-REACTIVE

Acrylic luminescent canvas painting, 40" × 60", made by ~VAS (V.G.). A meditation on the multi-dimensional nature of existence — two evolutionary pattern lines of a single creature, each side unfolding and evolving yet inseparable from the other whole. Painted with UV-reactive pigments that reveal a second layer of meaning under ultraviolet light.

// DRAG THE SLIDER TO COMPARE NATURAL vs UV LIGHT

"I find it interesting how we all get wrapped up in our perspective of life — that the animistic part of us is slowly ripped apart from the need to compete for survival in this modern world." — ~VAS, on Loop of Life

What you see here are two evolutionary pattern lines for this creature — how each side unfolds and evolves. The fact that they are so close and each part of the 'one' whole makes them practically inseparable. Existence in its own means being: to be, what you represent, is a form of creation within another form of creation and so on. The UV layer reveals the hidden dimension of the work — the animistic undercurrent beneath the visible surface.

Worlds of Color

// ACRYLIC LUMINESCENT · UV-REACTIVE · PYTHAGOREAN MORPHOLOGY

Two complimenting unities of "worlds in colors" merged with Pythagorean Cosmic morphology. The artwork presents personality as color — mapping the Big Five personality traits (Openness, Conscientiousness, Extroversion, Agreeableness, Neuroticism) onto an ever-lasting pattern of being that implies both existence and absence simultaneously. UV-reactive pigments again reveal a second dimensional layer.

Life is a mystery — an ever-lasting pattern of being which implies both to be and not to be at the same time. Your personality consists of traits that shape your presence: the "Big Five" characteristics of every human's behaviour — Openness, Conscientiousness, Extroversion, Agreeableness, Neuroticism. This artwork presents two complimenting unities of worlds in colors, merged with Pythagorean Cosmic morphology, asking what it means to carry all five dimensions simultaneously.
