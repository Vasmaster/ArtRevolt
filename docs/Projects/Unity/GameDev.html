Overliver

Serious Game project for training habits of healthy meal-preps


A serious game project made with GameLab Oost, in Enschede. The project was produced in collaboration with a faculty of MST. Intended to promote and be used by patients suffering from NAFLD (Non-alcoholic fatty Liver disease). Through brainstorms and proposals with the team and stakeholders, my work was to create the interactive elements(aestetics) to match the style determined by the Lead Artist. Additionally I came up with a recipe book within the UI. writen functions in C# and Unity UI structure. (lost archive)
