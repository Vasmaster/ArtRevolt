Speedboat — WallyPower 80

Hard-surface model inspired by real-life reference of the WallyPower 80 series. Training production focused on hard-surface modelling and realistic vehicle mechanics including buoyancy simulation. The model was used to develop physical behavior on a water surface, with a custom water shader built using Unreal Engine's Material Editor and Material Functions.
