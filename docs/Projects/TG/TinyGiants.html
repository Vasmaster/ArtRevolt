Hänsel und Gretel Opera

// NETHERLANDS REISOPERA · LIVE 3D PROJECTION · FANTASY OPERA

Real-time 3D projection visuals for the Netherlands Reisopera's fantasy opera production, accessible for audiences aged 10+. Breathtaking projected environments — from the enchanted forest intro to the dramatic staircase sequences and the swan lake finale — driven live alongside the performance. Music by Engelbert Humperdinck, sung in German with Dutch subtitles.

Unreal Engine 5 Real-time Rendering Live Production 3D Projection Mapping

Bond of Union

// CONCEPTUAL CG · M.C. ESCHER INTERPRETATION · PERSONAL PRODUCTION

A personal CG interpretation of M.C. Escher's "Bond of Union", blending Guido Moretti's sculptural style with Escher's illusory geometry. Conceptualised, designed and rendered entirely by me during my time at TinyGiants with their creative open-door invitation. The animation exploits shifting vantage points — side and front — to reveal distinct personalities within a single unified form.

Unreal Engine 5 OTOY Octane Cinema 4D After Effects Conceptual Art

AOC Keyboard Animation

// PRODUCT ANIMATION · C4D SIMULATION · OCTANE RENDER · COMPOSITING

Reworked and personalised the AOC keyboard presentation originally produced by the TinyGiants team for their client. I adapted the keyboard layout across the keys, then animated and rendered several sequences — exploring Cinema 4D's simulation tools and complex multi-pass compositing in After Effects. A pivotal production for levelling up in color-space management and project organisation in Ae.

Muscle Car Showcase

// ANIMATION · RENDER PASS COMPOSITING · GUIDED PRODUCTION

Provided with a complete scene, materials, and lights by TinyGiants, I was tasked with creating a fun, driven animation and compositing the render passes and shots in Adobe After Effects. Extremely educational — it brought my understanding of proper color spaces and project management in Ae up to professional standard.
