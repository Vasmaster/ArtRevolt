Bond of Union

Illusory artistic interpretation of the artwork Bond of Union from M.C. Escher

Personal production

During my work for TinyGiants with their guidance and open invitation to dive into something new, creative and interesting subject for me. I conceptualized, designed and rendered an animation of Escher's artwork. My spin on it was a combination of Guido Moretti style and Bond of Union. This was an atempt to create a new style of an illusion with the side and front vantage points to perceive different personality of my artwork.


AOC Keyboard Animation
